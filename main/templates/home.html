About KishanAi

KishanAi is an intelligent farming assistant built to help small and medium-scale farmers make faster, data-driven decisions. By combining lightweight computer-vision models, simple agricultural heuristics and practical recommendations, KishanAi brings modern precision-agriculture tools to everyday farming workflows — from early disease detection to livestock health checks and crop planning.

Our goal is to reduce crop and livestock losses, save time for farmers, and increase productivity by providing clear, actionable guidance through a simple web interface. KishanAi focuses on accessibility, low-cost deployment (works with a standard webcam or smartphone), and clear explainability so farmers understand both the problem and the recommended remedy.

KishanAi in a nutshell: AI-assisted disease detection, practical next-steps, and simple crop planning — all from a browser.

  • Quick on-device image checks (works with a webcam ).
  • Lightweight models designed for low-bandwidth environments.
  • Record-and-track captures for follow-up or expert review.

Plant Disease Detection

Upload plant images or use the live camera to detect diseases and get treatment recommendations.

Problem

Farmers often discover diseases too late — after significant leaf/fruit damage — because visual symptoms can be subtle at first. Early-stage infections spread quickly and are hard to diagnose without experience, leading to yield loss and unnecessary pesticide use.

Solution (what KishanAi provides)

  • Automated image-based detection of common plant diseases using a lightweight CNN model.
  • Live camera stream with real-time overlay of predicted disease label so farmers can inspect plants continuously.
  • Actionable advice and next steps (e.g., isolation, organic remedies, when to consult an agronomist).
  • Periodic capture feature to save documented evidence for follow-up and tracking.

Cow Skin Disease Detection

Upload an image of your cow to check skin health and get recommended steps.

Problem

Livestock skin conditions (parasites, infections, wounds) are common and can rapidly affect animal welfare and productivity. Small holders may miss early signs or apply incorrect treatments, increasing risk and veterinary costs.

Solution (what KishanAi provides)

  • Image-based screening to identify common cow skin issues and categorize severity.
  • Confidence score with explanations so farmers know how certain the model is.
  • Practical advice (isolate animal, cleaning, topical treatment, consult veterinarian) tailored to the predicted condition.
  • Option to keep records of images and predictions for vet appointments.

Crop Suggestion

Get crop recommendations based on soil nutrients, weather, and local conditions.

Problem

Choosing the right crop for specific soil and climatic conditions can be difficult. Poor crop selection reduces yields and income. Farmers lack simple, localized guidance that factors in soil nutrients, pH, climate and rainfall patterns.

Solution (what KishanAi provides)

  • A predictive recommendation engine that suggests suitable crops given N-P-K values, pH, temperature, humidity and rainfall.
  • Practical suggestions ranked by suitability and estimated factors to monitor.
  • Helps farmers plan crop cycles for better yield and reduced risk.

KishanAi is intended to provide guidance and early warnings — it does not replace professional veterinary or agronomy advice. For critical issues, consult a certified expert.
